What are Macronutrients?

Macronutrients are the nutrients that our body needs in abundance.These are fats,carbs and protein.

Acceptable Macronutrients Distrubition Range
  • 45-65% of your daily intake from carbs. Best sources are fruits,veggies and whole foods.
  • 20-35% of your daily intake from fat. Bests sources are fish like salmon and tuna, chicken and even avacados.
  • 10-35% of your daily intake from protein. Best sources of complete proteins are meat, eggs and vegan sources like tofu and quinoa.
The Healthy Plate Model

The healthy plate model is simple, break your plate into quarters. One half your plate fruits & veggies, one quarter whole grains and one quarter protein.
